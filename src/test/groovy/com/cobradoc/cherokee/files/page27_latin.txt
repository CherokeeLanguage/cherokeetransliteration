2. Tali
Tsuganowv itsa dunvdasv tsvsgino iyusdi, uyoiyv, astayv
ganvgoisdi dusugv, unalehanoho anisgaya yiwuniyvla,
hanagwo widaniyohosgo. Utsatinv itsa, degaduhv nahnadegesv,
tsunaditasdi nole uniyo anigeya duninelv nole galogwe gvti dinatli
nole ayelasdi gvti dinatli. Gesdi tsusginoyigi. Nasgi tsusgino.
Sgino nusdidanv uwagadidisvi. Gesdi yitsigowatisge itsa
oginvgoisdi. Gesdi osda adanvti hanitsa wigagananvgovsdi yigese.
Aseno yeligwo wigananvgoisdi adehosida nole ulidastanv gvhanv
igohida, dikanohedi yigesvna dikanohedi ase aguyvsdeda yigi.
Agvyitsa uwasa wiguwanilohisdi. Yona gayula niduwugotano
udaniyasdi dunvdasv, gesdi gohosdi. Guwasdedaliyvstanedi
yigese, tsa aniwatsini uninetsv ale ditse ditsilostanv igvnadenv
ale diyostanv dastanvhanv. Dunvdasvyeno uwenvsv, sginana
nusdesdi. Ayatenv gado yiwuwedola Yona, tsudigaloosiso elo
nulistanehe, gesdiyeno gohosdi guwalewasdododi yigi, usiwagwo.
Adisgv kanigidv dunvdasv yvdoha, navnige dagwalela tsagugilo
gedaliya itsa ditsoyi yigesvna gedali itsa.
Tsigeyui sgina udvsonv nole nelisgv itsiyadvhanedi, nole
agwohiyuhv Yona tsutseli aniyvwi anelisgvgwo unadati, nasgiya
aniso aniyvwi.
