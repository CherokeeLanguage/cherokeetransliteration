5. Hisgi

Agwaduli asgwadisgv Tsali utseli kanohedv, uwodu gowelanv
nole kanohesgi nuyoiyv ulosv, aseno gesdi gohosdi kanohedagwo
ale uyoi yigeso nasgiyv nigalisdisgv. Tsulosv kanohedv nigalisdisgv,
asiyvwi gesv, uwadoliisdi uwasa. Yvwi agitliyodi unilohisdi nole
daniyohosgo, yanigatahvna gesv nole gegatsigonelv.
Uligohvsdi, alisgwadisgv ganohilidasdi nvdo, agwosahisanv
nulistanelv Tsali, gvginohiselv Nenisi, Lowani, Tsatsi nole Tsegi.
Nole nudale yeli usvhi iyv atsilvyulvdi doginotlv Tsali. Ulisgedv
agiyelvhv, hisgi itsudalega agwadvganv nulistanidolv, gesdi
tsusoyvha yigese, agwolitsv tsiyugodi nulistanv.
Gesdi unadanvtelv yigese tsunihisdi. Uhayalvna unadisgalodi
Asamida gesdi yiduwadv galuyasti, anelisge anisgaya gesdi ase
uninohisedi yigese. Ugitsvda sunale, unadvnvistana tsuniyelvsdi,
dunvsgatlane galuyasti dunahanuwv hawinitsa, ohane iyv
tsunvdodi digostayi digvdodi unelise.
