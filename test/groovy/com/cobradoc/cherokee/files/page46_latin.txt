3. Tsoi

Agwenvsv Waya, uhisodi ugedaliyv wagilosv, nigada elo
Tsalistaluyasgo agiyelvhv. Wilimi Galuyasdi agiwadvhv
wagilutsagwo. Ayeli ayvwiya, ogatseligo doye ahi. Ulisgedv
uyelvhi aginohiselv guwateholv unalidigidv. Atsilvyulvdi nav
doginanv agwadvgastanv kanohesgv.
Udasinidenv wunilutse Galuyasdi, dunahanuwv,
dunistiyegv, uninegalv asvge tsunilosv - anisvge uhayesadv
tsugwalogv, gosta, inage anehi tsunadigida, ali nole ada
tsugasvsdi. Dayunanigidv tsunenvsv nvdo ayeli digeyv, geyv
tsunistawadvse gvnigesdv ugedaliyv tsunilose, tsegwa nvya
dugasadv ama geyv, unega ama galosgi ayeli dugasadvi nvya,
nole galvladi atsohayv, iga iyv gila agaliho. Eladi ugedaliyv
uninanvgotse, hana dunigohe dunvdasv digadogi sagonige
tsuyvtsv itsa, navnige atsohayv gvnigesv igvne alisgwadisgv elo.
Hilvhitsv kalvgv itsa anaisv, dunisotse atsilov, hana tsunihiye
tsalagiyi ayeli amayeli uniyvle, unalidagidv nunalistane.
Talidu iyani anisgaya, anigeya nole diniyotli. Wai
unanelotanv Tsali dudatihnav.
