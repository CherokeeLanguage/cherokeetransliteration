Dadanenulvdisge nole dayostanehe yvwi. Gesdi unitayolv
yigese, uhayole. Galosidohe dunvdasv dikanogisge, uwodu gese
osda yadvgastanvna yigi nigawesgv, kanohesgi yvwi tsuwelisiya
digisdi. Digatisdi gayesadi duwasadohaye unadadasgisodi
nole uniwadvdi unihisdi, aseno hilvhiyu tsigese usoyi gese.
Dunadasv unigehvse, nogwo unasgose atalegasv unisadvdi.
Tsisgwa uninohisele iyv unalisosdi gatladi, ayeli duwatsisgatlv
nvya unvnisti udanvdo. Uwadoliisdi unadatalose yvwi, hilvhiyu
tsigese usoyi gese. Gesdino yuhiya unasuyagisdi.
Asgwadonv tsinohesgv, agigatidv. Hisgi iyuwagvti uwahanilv
adanvdo. Gvnigesv gvhanihv agwoyenv nole tsileni galvladitsv.
Tsidunile aniyosgi, usoyi adanenulvdisgi nigada digetsiisdi
digiyvwi. Nole usoyi, gegasuyagisdi yigesvna.
Ulisgwadonv, nigada utsaleda tsutseli yvwi wogilutsv
waya. Tsotsaisv, dotsutagistanv ohani anegi unalidagidv.
Ogalistayvnona sgadugi tsulasgi yotsalewisdisgvna tali ale tsoi
tsudodagwa, utsaleda tsutseli anisgaya ogaligonv otsihayalvsv
Tsali. Degeyv dotsihayalv nasgiya gitli tsidanihayoho tsula.
Tsali nole ulitinav unaiidagise nasgiya getsisahadohayv awi ale
gwehi unatliwidvdi aninohilido itsa.
