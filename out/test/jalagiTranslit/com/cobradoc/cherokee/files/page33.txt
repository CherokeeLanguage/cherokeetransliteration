Uwanvdv tsalagiyi ayeli nole navni datsilov tsuyvtsv itsa
gayalenv:
Gadvnvvsdisgv kanohesgi atsilodv, osda tsayelvdi hadelohosv
nusdidanv ayeli dogiluwistanvha, detsinasav tsunowelanv
digilutselv nole agwvsa agwadelohosv. Ayeli nusdv nole doye
nusdidanv astayi atsilosdi nigalistanidohv, nole elisdi staya
nunalistane.
Anistawadisv detsinvwistanv, gesdi adelohohisdi yigi naniv
aniyvwiya hani sgadugi, nole unistawadvsdi kanohedv datsohisdv
gegvwoodi. Agvyi advdvnv naniv, unatlinigvgv, unisoyasdisgi gesv
anehv, unalistayvdi. Gayotli datsinoheli nunadvnadegv aniyvwiya
sginana uyelvdi.
Uyo iyunadvnadegi, yanigatahvna adela unosvdi, aniwisvvsgi
unalistayvdi, ohisdodi awi unigehvsdi nole awi nudanulvhv
esgagwo adela unosvdi. Agada unilvgwodi anehv aniyvwiya, gesdi
yanigata nusdidanv anehv doye, yunadulisgvna nelagi unelisdi
deganisahv tsunigayvli, nasgi unohiyuhv. Anadadoligi gvhanoda
nanvne anitayohihv gegvwoodi yigesvna, nole yidaninohisehvna
gesv uhisdodi gesv unadanvtelv. Iyanadvne unigvwiyuhi nole
igada aniyuwanega unadasuyv, danidoligv ale unatselvhvgwo,
anisadoyvsga atsutanv nusdv unadulisgv aniwatsini.
