Sega alewistisgv advgastisge iyv agvyitsa alitelvhesgv
tsugwalogv. Yusgwalotselv advlisgv, uskanola aise, nigohilvna
nageiyv agvyitsa nudvnadise yona. Ulewistane udvgastane,
etlawehi gese, egwa utalone osda udvgodi. Yeligwo ustawadvsdi
dudeyotanv tsugwaloga nole tsulisgwalida duwanigatlv nole
giga, kanigida nulistanv ulosvi, agvyitsa uwenvse elisgvgwo
iyudvneti yona nustidanv inagei udisgalodi. Iyudaliha awatisge
giga udaneha gado, ayatenv udanvliyev uyalugv, nelisgv
nigalisdisge.
Iga svheyitsa igohida ustawadvse ulosv yona, yalewistisgvna
kanalusgv. Gayotlige nusde giga. Alewistisge atasgisgv ale
adanvwisge.
Deganehv tsuwasgwolv nidegalisdisge. Tsuwasgwolv tsusdi
gigage nvya iyusdi dudanehe tsugwalogv. Dulasgenv gado
usgolvige nigalisdisge, nasgiya galvlo itsa tsalisaladisgo.
Usganoli adasise Tsali, eladi nudvnadise uhayohv ulosv.
Unvgotse dugedaliyv, tsulisuwida tsugwaloga degadav detsugv,
agadidv yona utsesdo nole galegi udvsv goge, galiwoge
agowatisgv iyudanvhida, nole tsukahayosv dunvdasv, usvhehi
unaluse adohi detsugv kanigida tsugwaloga, gvnige usgolv
gola iyusdi notsiyu itsa gvnige duwusav galvladige dunvdasv,
elohi udayvlati nole etlawehi, dalasgesgv advgodi yigesvna
wanai galageyv notsi uwohosv. Gesdi yudulisge widuwasohisdi
sginana atsilov, sogwo iyuwagvta duwasotsv udvna ugugu
egwa asgola etlawehi unohilvstane agvyitsa ayeli ulisigv
notsi hawinitsa. Uwasa ugadv igvda gese ugugu ugadv nole
usgolv, nasgiya nvdo. Uyoiyu. Igawesdi yagudulisgvna advgodi
aguwasa yvdo gadusi.
Gadusi unaluse Tsali, sogwo iyuwasgwolv iyudali, kanigidv
winigalisgi agvyitsa. Agvyi itsa ustawadvse utlvyolihv igohida,
nogwo ulisgwadv ugohv uwasgwolv giga nole udehayalidole,
gohosti yawatisgvna. galvladi detsuga uhayale nole galvlii
witsitsei, hilvhitsv wutliwidvhe.
