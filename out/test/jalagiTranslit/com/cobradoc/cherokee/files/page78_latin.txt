4. Nvgi
Ugitsvda nulistanidolv tsudalenvda uninohelv nole unowelanv
ohane dudetiyvsadisv. Igadano aninohesgv, yagwaligonvna aniyosgi
nole aniyvwiya unanigisv nvno, gegweyotanv gohosti yinvgalista.
Anisoi anadisge uyo agwadanvdv, agwadaniyv amayi degasosgv
notsi tsugv hawinitsv, agwvsa gadanvtesgv nusdv nagwadvnelv.
Tsuyugoti yitsinohvla, gesdiha osda yinagwalistane. Agwanvdv, yeli
gohidv ogilitsowatanv adohi aniyosgi, dagiyawetsv aniyosgi uwasa
ogaligonv. anigeya ogaligoti agwadulisgv. gelisgv ayeli udodagwada
wigalohisdi Welisi tsunaditasde, detsigatahv tsusgwala deganvnv
nole osda sogwili agilvtanv. Asamida datihanehv anaisv, yeligwo
agwatsanvdadehv susvhida ogaligodi Welisi dunasav anigeya,
ugitsvda widigatsvdodi. sgino osda agwanvdv, iyusti unagili
aniyosge nole anegi unisganvtsv sunale tsinigalisdi tsananigisge,
Asamida usgaida utane usdi goweli digwatsilostanv atsilodv.
Sgino iyusdi ugitsvda gila wagilutsv getsilv gado, gado nusdv
agwadelohosv nulistanidolv.
